My Story

My name is Rudy Victorio, owner and operator of Fine Entrances Gate and Metal Fabrications. We are a local family business that takes the time to get to know you and your needs. I bring to my customers 30 years of experience in multiple applications of welding and metal fabrication as a Certified Structural Welder. If you are curious, I'm proficient in TIG for Mild Steel, Stainless Steel, Duplex, Titanium and Aluminum, MIG for Mild Steel,Flux, and Arc for Structural Welding.

In my practice, I specialise in metal entry gates and fencing that can be embellished with ornamental designs as well as wood panels to customize the look you are seeking to beautify your home. Due to my respectable range of capabilities, I am well versed in custom building single or double axel trailers for lawn busses or small toy haulers used for motorcycles and ATV's.
